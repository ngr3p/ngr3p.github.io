About_

I'm Leo, aka ngr3p — this blog is a personal initiative to explore offensive security with intention, focus, and consistency.

I’m driven by curiosity. I explore systems to understand how they work, where security flaws exist, and how they can be corrected and improved to become more resilient.

Everything you see here is part of my learning process — documenting, practicing, reflecting, repeating. This blog is my personal repository. Sharing what I learn is part of my mission. If it helps someone else along the way, then it’s already worth it.

If you're on this journey too — welcome. Stay focused. Stay sharp. Keep breaking things, and document everything.
